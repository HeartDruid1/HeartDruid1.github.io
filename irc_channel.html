How to connect

By using your preferred IRC client (I prefer Pidgin or HexChat) follow the instructions below

1) Connect to the Foonetic server, more info on this can be found here

1.5) Pick a nickname that people will see when you join the room (although don't pick HeartDruid1 as that is me and it's a registered nickname. You can try but I have the upper hand)

2) Join the HeartDruid1 chat by typing in '/join #heartdruid1'
